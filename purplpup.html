About

"PURPL PUP" is a personal brand for games which I have developed almost completely on my own. It can be considered as my Game Development Alias.

I like to make games in teams as well as on my own to develop my skills. Sometimes I enjoy the freedom and challenge of working without the strengths of others. It has allowed me to develop skills such as Art, and Music, which benefits my communication abilities when working with Artists and Musicians, and helps provide context for their abilities.
