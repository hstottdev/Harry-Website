Hi, I’m Harry – a passionate Game Developer, Web Developer, and Artist

I love creating games and short solo projects on my own, as well as working with others as collaboration is the key to a better final product.

I moved to London to pursue a degree in Game Development at ACM, where I graduated with a First-Class Honours. It was here that I was lucky to discover a true passion for programming, development, and design, and I’ve found myself wanting to do it every minute of the day.

I thrive on the creativity and problem-solving aspects of development, finding it both engaging and deeply rewarding. I’m highly self-motivated, always eager to learn new techniques and dive into research whenever I take on a new project.
