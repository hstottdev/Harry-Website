Overview

'Arjybeasts' is a Turn-Based RPG in which players draw their own characters and fight using the stats of their chosen RGB values. Image files on your computer refuse to be deleted! Yet, you must find a way to fight them with your image (or Arjybeast) to gain storage and install 'Rabid Dog Simulator'.

This started as a solo passion project which I created for a university project, and soon became something a bit more ambitious. Now, still a passion project with a small team of 3, the game will be developed in full, potentially picking up more team members along the way.
