About

Slimey Hatch Studios is an Indie Game Development studio founded by me (Harry Stott) and Samuel Glancey in 2021.

At Slimey Hatch, we take wacky games seriously. We're professionals at having a laugh, and masters of Unity and C#. We were one of the 19 Teams in the UK to be selected for Tranzfuser 2024, where we're now developing a game called 'Arjybeasts'.

What started as something to show our passion outside of university, has quickly become a professional game studio. We're launching 'Arjybeasts' for Steam, and previously launched our own mobile game called 'Banana For Scale'.

I'm responsible for much of the vision at Slimey Hatch Studios. Others, such as Sam, are responsible for putting my vision into practice, and sometimes, reining me in a bit.

Let's not forget Kristian, our super talented Musican and Programmer extraordinare, who is just as much the team as myself and Sam.
